Shalina Prakash

I'm currently an AI Policy Fellow at Georgia Tech, where I work on labour shocks in developing economies, and a Research Fellow at Sentient Futures, where I work on AI's implications for foundational questions in ethics, philosophy of mind and political philosophy. Recently, I graduated from the University of St Andrews, where I read Philosophy and English. Whilst at university, I interned with the UNDP in India, followed by the GICJ in Geneva and a very brief spell at the Herald in Scotland.
